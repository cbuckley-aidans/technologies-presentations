What is a Civil Engineer?
Definition
Civil engineers design, construct, manage and maintain the infrastructure of modern society.
What they work on
Roads, railways, tunnels, buildings, bridges, airports, mines, dams, ports and harbours, water supply and sewerage systems, and flood mitigation works.
In short: civil engineering is about solving problems for the public.
What is an Architect?
Definition
Architects design, plan and oversee the construction of buildings and spaces for human use.
What they work on
Homes, schools, hospitals, museums, offices, libraries, theatres, public plazas and urban precincts.
In short: architecture is about designing spaces for people to live, work and gather in.
Which is which?
1
Primarily responsible for how a building is designed and experienced.

Focuses on spatial layout, aesthetics, functionality for the people inside it, and compliance with planning and building codes.

Training is heavily oriented toward design thinking, human experience of space, and visual communication.

The lead designer and the client's primary point of contact on a building project.
2
Primarily responsible for whether the structure physically works and is safe.

Focuses on loads, forces, foundations, structural integrity, and the physical systems that make a building or piece of infrastructure stand up and function.

Training is heavily mathematical and physics-based.
